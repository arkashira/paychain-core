# customer-journey.md

## Customer Journey Map for paychain-core

### 1. Aware
- **Trigger Event:** Awareness of high transaction fees and slow processing times in current payment systems.
- **Friction Points:** Lack of understanding of blockchain technology; skepticism about decentralization.
- **User Emotions:** Frustration, curiosity, skepticism.
- **Opportunities to Delight:** Educational content (webinars, whitepapers) explaining blockchain benefits; case studies showcasing successful implementations.
- **Metric:** Number of website visits and engagement with educational content.

### 2. Consider
- **Trigger Event:** Researching alternatives to existing payment processing systems.
- **Friction Points:** Overwhelming information; difficulty comparing traditional systems with decentralized options.
- **User Emotions:** Confusion, cautious optimism, hope.
- **Opportunities to Delight:** Interactive comparison tools; personalized consultations to address specific needs.
- **Metric:** Number of inquiries and demo requests.

### 3. Try
- **Trigger Event:** Signing up for a trial or demo of paychain-core.
- **Friction Points:** Technical setup challenges; initial learning curve associated with blockchain.
- **User Emotions:** Excitement, anxiety, determination.
- **Opportunities to Delight:** Comprehensive onboarding support; user-friendly documentation and tutorials.
- **Metric:** Trial conversion rate and user engagement during the trial period.

### 4. Adopt
- **Trigger Event:** Successful implementation of paychain-core in the business workflow.
- **Friction Points:** Integration with existing systems; potential resistance from staff.
- **User Emotions:** Relief, satisfaction, confidence.
- **Opportunities to Delight:** Ongoing support and training; showcasing early successes and ROI.
- **Metric:** Adoption rate and user satisfaction scores.

### 5. Expand
- **Trigger Event:** Recognition of benefits leading to interest in additional features or services.
- **Friction Points:** Uncertainty about scaling; potential costs of expansion.
- **User Emotions:** Enthusiasm, cautious optimism, concern about investment.
- **Opportunities to Delight:** Tailored expansion plans; success stories from similar businesses.
- **Metric:** Rate of upsells and cross-sells; customer lifetime value (CLV).
