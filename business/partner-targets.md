# partner-targets.md

## Partner Integration Roadmap for paychain-core

### Target Integrations

1. **Stripe**
   - **Rationale**: Stripe is a leading payment processing platform that offers a wide range of payment solutions. Integrating with Stripe can provide access to a large customer base and enhance the payment processing capabilities of paychain-core.
   - **Free-tier limits**: No free tier; transaction fees apply.
   - **Integration effort**: Medium
   - **Value-add**: Simplifies payment processing for businesses, allowing them to accept payments easily and securely.

2. **PayPal**
   - **Rationale**: PayPal is one of the most recognized payment platforms globally. Integrating with PayPal can enhance user trust and provide a familiar payment option for users.
   - **Free-tier limits**: No free tier; transaction fees apply.
   - **Integration effort**: Medium
   - **Value-add**: Facilitates quick and easy transactions for users, improving the overall user experience.

3. **Square**
   - **Rationale**: Square offers a comprehensive suite of payment solutions and is popular among small to medium-sized businesses. This integration can help reach a broader audience.
   - **Free-tier limits**: No free tier; transaction fees apply.
   - **Integration effort**: Medium
   - **Value-add**: Provides a seamless payment experience for merchants, allowing them to manage transactions efficiently.

4. **Coinbase Commerce**
   - **Rationale**: As a leading cryptocurrency exchange, Coinbase Commerce allows merchants to accept cryptocurrency payments. This integration aligns with the decentralized nature of paychain-core.
   - **Free-tier limits**: No monthly fees; transaction fees apply.
   - **Integration effort**: Small
   - **Value-add**: Enables businesses to accept digital assets, catering to the growing demand for cryptocurrency payments.

5. **Plaid**
   - **Rationale**: Plaid connects applications to users' bank accounts, facilitating seamless bank transfers and payments. This can enhance the functionality of paychain-core by providing direct bank integrations.
   - **Free-tier limits**: Free tier available with limited access to endpoints.
   - **Integration effort**: Medium
   - **Value-add**: Simplifies the process of linking bank accounts for users, improving transaction speed and reliability.

6. **Braintree**
   - **Rationale**: Braintree, a PayPal service, offers advanced payment solutions, including support for various payment methods. This integration can enhance flexibility for users.
   - **Free-tier limits**: No free tier; transaction fees apply.
   - **Integration effort**: Medium
   - **Value-add**: Provides a comprehensive payment solution that supports multiple currencies and payment types.

7. **Adyen**
   - **Rationale**: Adyen is a global payment company that allows businesses to accept payments in multiple currencies. Integrating with Adyen can enhance the international capabilities of paychain-core.
   - **Free-tier limits**: No free tier; transaction fees apply.
   - **Integration effort**: Large
   - **Value-add**: Streamlines global payment processing, making it easier for businesses to operate internationally.

8. **Revolut**
   - **Rationale**: Revolut offers banking services and allows users to hold and exchange multiple currencies. Integrating with Revolut can enhance the financial services aspect of paychain-core.
   - **Free-tier limits**: Free tier available with limited features.
   - **Integration effort**: Medium
   - **Value-add**: Provides users with a multi-currency wallet and payment options, enhancing user convenience.

### Prioritization Criteria
- Focus on integrations that provide affiliate/revenue-share opportunities.
- Prioritize platforms with established user bases to enhance market penetration.
- Consider the integration effort relative to the potential value-add for users.
